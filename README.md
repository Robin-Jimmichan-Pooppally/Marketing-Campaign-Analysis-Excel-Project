### Top ROI Channels

‚úÖ **Certain platforms** (e.g., Email or Social Media) achieved the highest ROI, delivering maximum value per dollar spent

‚úÖ **Paid Ad campaigns** (like Google Ads) showed high reach but comparatively lower ROI due to higher costs

‚úÖ **Moderate spending efficiency:** Campaigns with moderate spending often achieved better ROI than high-cost ones, suggesting budget efficiency matters more than scale

‚úÖ **Conversion rate impact:** Campaigns with higher engagement converted more efficiently, directly influencing overall ROI

### Underperforming Campaigns

‚úÖ **Negative ROI campaigns:** A few campaigns with high budgets but low revenue generated negative ROI, signaling the need for better targeting or creative adjustments

### Optimization Opportunity

‚úÖ **Budget reallocation potential:** Reallocating budget toward top-performing campaigns could potentially increase overall ROI by 10‚Äì15% based on observed trends
